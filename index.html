About Me

Hello! I am a detail-oriented and methodical project coordinator with experience in academic research and business analytics. I earned my bachelor's degree in psychology and linguistics from the University of California, San Diego in 2017. Since then, I have had the pleasure of working at multiple psychology laboratories. I am also a trained digital marketing analyst with years of experience in data wrangling for digital marketing purposes. It's my ambition to apply my skills in a dynamic, data-oriented environment - I am currently seeking a job!
