About Me

Hello! I earned my bachelor's degree in psychology and linguistics from the University of California, San Diego in 2017 and am currently attending graduate courses in statystical analysis at San Diego State University. In the last five years, I have had the pleasure of working at multiple psycholinguistics laboratories. My interest lies in understanding how language is represented in the mind, and how language production and comprehension are processed in relation to other cognitive functions. I am particularly drawn to the matter of meaning, and how it is encoded and retrieved during early stages of human development and beyond. I am actively seeking a research job in psychology and linguistics and hope to start grad school soon!
