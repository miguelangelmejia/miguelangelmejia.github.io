About Me

Hello! I am interested in understanding how language is represented in the mind, and how language production and comprehension are processed in relation to other cognitive functions. I am especially drawn to meaning, how it maps onto linguistic structure, and how it is encoded and retrieved in the mind. I earned my bachelor's degree in psychology and linguistics (with an emphasis on cognition) from the University of California, San Diego in 2017. Since then, I have had the pleasure of working at multiple laboratories, both as a research assistant and as a lab manager. It's my ambition to enter graduate school and begin my own research on the acquisition and development of concepts and event representations. I am currently seeking a lab job!
